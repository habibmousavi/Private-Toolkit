Jackey the Fool


Jackey the fool has an old shed. It has no windows, so it is very dark, and it is full of old things. One day, Jackey the fool goes into his shed to get a ladder, but slips on something and falls against a big garden fork. The fork hits him on the head and knocks him down. Then it falls on top of him and hits him hard on the left leg. The ends of the fork then go into his long beard. He fights with the fork fiercely, and at last throws it off him, jumps up and runs out of the shed. He is very angry. He has an old sword under his bed, and now he runs and gets it. Then he runs back to the shed, opens the door suddenly and shouts in a terrible voice: “All right, come out and fight, you and all other forks in the world! I’m not afraid of you!”
