Dangerous Bear


A man always goes to the same bar at the same time every day and asks for two glasses of beer. He drinks them and then asks for two more.

One day the man behind the bar says to him, ‘Why do you always ask for two glasses of beer? Why don’t you get one big glass instead?’

The man answers, ‘Because I do not like to drink alone. I drink with my friend.’

But a few days later the man comes in and asks only for one beer.

‘Oh,’ says the barman, ‘is your friend dead?’

‘Oh no,' says the man, ‘He is very well. This beer is for him. My doctor doesn’t want me to drink anymore because it is dangerous for me.’
