A Good Idea


Mrs. Jones has two sons. They are big, strong boys, but they are lazy. On Saturdays they don't go to school, and then their mother always says, 'Please cut the grass in the garden this afternoon, boys.' The boys don’t like it, but they always do it.

One day, somebody gives one of the boys a magazine, and he sees a picture of a beautiful lawn-mower in it. There is a seat on it, and there is a woman on the seat.

The boy takes the picture to his mother and brother and says to them, 'Look, that woman is sitting on the lawn-mower and driving it and cutting the grass. We want one of those.'

'One of those lawn-mower?' his mother asks.

‘No,’ the boy says. 'We want one of those women. She can cut the grass every week.'
