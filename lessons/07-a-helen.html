Helen's Glasses


Helen’s eyes are not very good, so she usually wears glasses. But when she is seventeen and she begins to go out with a young man, she never wears her glasses when she is with him. When he comes to the door to take her out, she takes her glasses off, and when she comes home again and he leaves, she puts them on.

One day, her mother says to her, “but Helen, why do you never wear your glasses when you are with Jim? He takes you to beautiful places in his car, but you don’t see anything.”

“Well, mother,” says Helen, “I look prettier to Jim when I am not wearing my glasses – and he looks better to me too! ”
