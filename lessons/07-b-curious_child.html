William and the dead man


William is sitting by a window in his house one day in the middle of the winter, when he hears women outside crying. He puts his head out of the window, and sees a lot of people coming towards his house. They are carrying a dead man, and the women are crying, “oh, why are you leaving us to go to a place without light and without food? It will be dark there and you will be cold and hungry. Nobody will look after you; nobody will be kind to you; and nobody will love you there!”

“My God!” says William to his wife, “They are talking about our house. They are bringing the dead man here! Quick, lock the door. Don’t let them in!”
