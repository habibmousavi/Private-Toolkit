Tea or Coffee?


A man is sitting in a café and having a drink when he calls the waitress over. He points to his cup.

“This stuff tastes funny. What do you call it? Coffee or tea?”

“What do you mean, sir?” says the waitress.”

“It tastes like paint,” says the man.

“Ah, well,” says the waitress. “if it tastes like paint, it must be coffee. Our tea tastes like soap.”
