smart student


One day a student is taking a very difficult essay exam. At the end of the test, the prof asks all the students to put their pencils down and immediately hand in their tests. The young boy keeps writing furiously, although the teacher warns him that if he does not stop immediately, he will disqualify him. He ignores the warning, finishes the test minutes later, and goes to hand the test to his instructor.

The instructor tells him he will not take the test. The student asks, “Do you know who I am? ”

The prof says, “No and I don’t care.”

The student asks again, “Are you sure you don’t know who I am? ”

The prof again says no. Therefore, the student walks to the pile of tests, places his in the middle, then throws the papers in the air. “Good” the student says, and walks out. He passes.
